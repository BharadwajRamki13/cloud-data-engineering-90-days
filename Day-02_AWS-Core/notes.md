S3 is preferred over local storage because it provides highly durable, low-cost, and infinitely scalable object storage that can be accessed by multiple services simultaneously, making it ideal for data lakes and large-scale data processing.


When would you choose EC2 instead of a managed service?
You would choose EC2 instead of a managed service when you need full control over the operating system, custom configurations, or when running open-source tools like Spark or Airflow that require specific setup and tuning.
“Managed services trade flexibility for simplicity, while EC2 offers maximum control.”

What problem does IAM solve in AWS?
IAM solves the problem of controlling and securing access to AWS resources by defining who can access which services and what actions they are allowed to perform, following the principle of least privilege.
IAM prevents unauthorized access and accidental misuse of resources.
